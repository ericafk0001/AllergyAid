Our Mission

At Allergy Aid, we believe everyone deserves safe, stress-free dining experiences. Our mission is to connect allergy-conscious diners with trusted restaurants that provide clear allergen information and thoughtfully crafted menus.

What We Do

We partner with local and national restaurants to offer accurate allergen data and filtering tools so you can confidently enjoy meals without worry. By supporting our partners, we build a safer dining community for people with food allergies.
